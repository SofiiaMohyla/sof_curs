    Women's pixie haircut

    A pixie haircut is a great solution for a contemporary woman on the go. It’s convenient, pretty and appropriate for hair of any type. Pixie haircuts for thick hair are full of texture and manageable volume. If your hair is fine, you may also choose the right pixie hair cut that adds dimension and boosts thickness of your short locks. Modern pixie cut styles are not limited to modest boyish ‘dos. You may indulge in a variety of pixie hairstyles with slicked back or tousled hair, try short pixie hairstyles for curly hair, asymmetrical vintage ‘dos or funky Faux Hawks. Short and long pixie haircuts with bangs are the most popular short hair choice nowadays. There are cute ideas of pixies for most face shape, including cute pixie cuts for round face. Have a look at these trendy short pixie haircuts and pick a new hot version to refresh your stylish look this season!

    Women's bob haircut

    Medium bob hairstyles are classic and classy. They can look very different depending on your cut and the way of styling. Wavy and straight, shaggy and sleek, asymmetrical and symmetrical bobs offer you the modern look, diversity and convenience you want from a hairstyle. Check our gallery of 60 mid-length bobs and pick the most appealing versions to try!

    Women's haircut in military style

    Serving in the Army requires a well-groomed and professional appearance. The Army strives to be as inclusive as possible while making sure every Soldier can perform their duties as safely as possible. Appearance regulations are considerate of religious beliefs and many forms of cultural expression.

    Women's haircut cap

    The cap haircut appeared in the 60s of the last century, and now it is gaining momentum again and will be one of the most popular this season. The key element of this form is long and expressive bangs. The best option is high and thick. For most hats, the bangs are taken directly from the top of the head and go to the eyebrows. Bangs to the middle of the forehead have also become fashionable in modern versions.

    Women's square haircut

    Kare can be of different lengths, textures, colors, be strict or feminine, flirtatious or elegant, everything is possible. Surely, scrolling through Instagram, you saw one of your favorite bloggers with a bob or watching a fashion show, or just walking around the city. Even almost all Hollywood celebrities cut their hair and wear their own version of the iconic bob at some point. Today we're going to find out what's so special about this simple haircut that feels timeless and classic. And as the bob is constantly being updated to offer a variety of cut lengths and textures, we'll also discuss bob fashion trends for 2023 along with classic types that have stood the test of time.

    Women's haircut lisenka

    Ladder haircut is very popular with the beautiful half of humanity. This is because this hairstyle is unpretentious and does not need daily styling. Moreover, it can be done on both medium and long hair. Also, this hairstyle looks equally advantageous on curly hair and on straight ones. From this article you will learn what the modern version of the haircut looks like, and to whom it suits.

    Women's haircut ladder

    Someone thinks that haircuts ladder and cascade This is the same. Or that these are either two names of the same model, or a common name and more scientific. But still it's not like that - differences between them are significant, and we will consider them in this article. These two haircuts are quite versatile, suitable for both long and short hair, for different face shapes, for long or curly curls. These hairstyles are well suited for those who do not want to shorten the length, but want to change the external image. Even if these models have several similar features, they are still different haircuts. They have differences, and the technique of execution also has its own characteristics and nuances.

    Women's torn haircut

    For owners of short hair, a torn haircut or torn haircut provides a unique opportunity for experimentation. You can create a creative image with the help of geometric or asymmetrical cuts, long bangs, tousled short nape. A torn haircut is universal and suitable for different types of hair. Unlike the classic cascade or ladder, it does not have a smooth transition between the strands, and the hair is cut not along an oblique line, but in a straight line, with a perfectly even cut. The most advantageous version of rvanka is done on short hair.

    Women's haircut straight cut

    This hairdo is a mix between the layered cut and the shredded cut. It has choppy layers with disconnected ends of different lengths. An ideal choice for a sassy and modern look, the Shag haircut looks equally stunning on both thin and thick hair. To add some flair to the Shag, you can style it with curtain bangs or frayed fringe. Great haircut for flaunting your long straight hair.
